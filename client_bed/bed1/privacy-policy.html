Privacy Policy of Shanthibhavan Bed Donation Program

Shanthibhavan Palliative Hospital is committed to protecting the privacy of adults living in communities where we work. As part of this commitment, we strictly prohibit the use of photographs on this website for any other purpose, personal or commercial.

We are equally committed to protecting the privacy of our donors, supporters, employees, registered members, and other stakeholders. We value the trust you place in us and recognize that maintaining this trust requires transparency and accountability in how we treat your personal information.

During the course of our activities, we frequently gather and use personal information. Anyone from whom we collect such information should expect it to be carefully protected and used only with express or implied consent. Shanthibhavan Palliative Hospital adheres to best practices for the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information as outlined in this policy.

Information of Personal Nature

Shanthibhavan Palliative Hospital is the owner of the Bed Donation Program and the website https://shanthibhavan.in/. We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is collected and used on our application and website. By accessing our services, you agree to the collection and use of your data in the manner provided here.

What information is collected?

We may request personal information during donations, onboarding, or other interactions with our program:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Address
  • Mobile Number
  • Location
  • Donation amount and payment details (processed securely)

How is the information collected?

We collect information only as part of a voluntary donation process, program registration, or direct communication with our team.

Will the information be shared?

We do not rent, sell, or share your personal information. Your financial details are used only to complete your donation transaction. Information may be used to:

  • Send updates or acknowledgments for your donation
  • Investigate or prevent unlawful activities, suspected fraud, or security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations or law enforcement requests

Information protection

We implement physical, electronic, and managerial safeguards to protect your data. Our servers are accessible only to authorized personnel, and your information is shared internally strictly on a need-to-know basis. However, data transmitted over the Internet cannot be guaranteed completely secure. By using our services, you agree that we will not be liable for any unauthorized disclosures caused by transmission errors or third-party actions.

Policy updates

Shanthibhavan Bed Donation Program reserves the right to update this policy at any time. Updates take effect immediately upon being posted on our website.
